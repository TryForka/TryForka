How Forka Started

2024 – THE IDEA

A packed schedule, a stressed family, and a real problem.

We built Forka because we lived it. Juggling sports, school, and packed family schedules, we watched the same stressful question come up every single night: "what's for dinner?" We weren't alone — 53% of consumers say it's a major daily pain point. So we got to work building the solution.

EARLY 2025 – BUILT FORKA

The engine that changed everything.

Forka's first engine launched, capable of building personalized weekly meal plans in under 20 seconds. Early testers called it "the only meal planner that actually gets my family."

TODAY – HELPING FAMILIES

From one family's problem to every family's solution.

Forka is built by a team of 5, including two co-founders, on a shared mission to take the stress out of weekly meal planning. We're the one-stop shop for planning, shopping, and cooking smarter, every single week. And we're just getting started.
