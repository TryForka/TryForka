FEATURE 01

Forka builds your weekly plan in seconds

Tell Forka your dietary preferences, allergies, family size, and weekly schedule. Forka generates a complete 7-day meal plan personalized to exactly your needs in under 20 seconds.

  • Accounts for picky eaters
  • Balances nutrition automatically
  • Adapts to your weekly schedule

FEATURE 02

Your grocery list, automatically.

Every ingredient from your weekly plan compiles into one smart shopping list. Forka portions everything for your serving size and groups items by aisle so shopping takes minutes, not hours.

  • Auto-portioned to your servings
  • Grouped by store aisle
  • Edit, remove, or add items in one tap

FEATURE 03

Step-by-step cooking that doesn't suck.

Forka walks you through every recipe one step at a time, with built-in timers, ingredient checklists, and serving size adjustments built right into the cooking screen.

  • Built-in step timers
  • Tap to check off ingredients
  • Auto-scaled to your serving size

From plan to pantry in one tap.

Send your full grocery list to your favorite store with a single tap. No retyping, no app-switching, no friction. Forka connects directly to major grocery services so your cart is ready before you even leave the house.

  • One-tap grocery cart send
