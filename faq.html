Getting Started

How do I sign up for Forka? +
Just download the app or sign up at tryforka.com, answer a few quick questions about your dietary preferences and family size, and you're ready to go.
Is Forka available on iOS and Android? +
Yes! Forka is available on both the App Store and Google Play.
Do I need a credit card to start? +
No. The 2-week free trial requires no payment info to begin.

Meal Plans

How does the AI build my meal plan? +
Forka analyzes your dietary preferences, allergies, household size, weekly schedule, and past recipe ratings to generate a personalized 7-day plan in under 10 seconds.
Can I customize my meal plan? +
Absolutely. Swap any meal, lock favorites, regenerate single days, or build a plan completely from scratch.
What dietary restrictions does Forka support? +
Vegan, vegetarian, keto, paleo, gluten-free, dairy-free, low-carb, Mediterranean, pescatarian, halal, kosher, and major allergies.
How far ahead can I plan? +
Plan up to 4 weeks at a time.

Pricing & Billing

How much does Forka cost? +
$9.99/month after a 2-week free trial. Cancel anytime.
Can I cancel my subscription? +
Yes. Cancel anytime from your account settings.
Will I be charged automatically after the trial? +
Only if you've added a payment method. We'll always remind you 3 days before the trial ends.
Do you offer refunds? +
If you're unsatisfied within the first 30 days of paid service, contact us for a full refund.

Cooking & Recipes

How many recipes does Forka have? +
Over 50,000 recipes from trusted sources, all with full nutrition info, photos, and step-by-step instructions.
Can I save my favorite recipes? +
Yes. Tap the heart icon on any recipe to add it to your favorites.
Does the cook mode keep my screen on? +
Yes. While in cook mode, your screen stays on so you never get locked mid-recipe.
Can I add my own recipes? +
Custom recipe import is coming soon. For now, you can favorite and rate any recipe in our library.
